Hello! I am Grace Kissel.

I have been making art using Blender since around 2018. Much of my work focuses on empty, "liminal" spaces. I also occasionally create outdoor spaces as well as urban environments. I use BlenderKit for many of the models in my renders. Currently, I am using the latest release of Blender 4.0. If you are interested in commissions, please see the "Contact" page.
