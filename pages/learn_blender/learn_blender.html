Welcome to the "Learn Blender" page!

This page will be updated with my own tutorials and guides! For now, I have added a few tutorials that were especially helpful to me when I first started using Blender for art.

Blender Donut Tutorials

Many people are familiar with Blender Guru's beginner tutorials. They are what began my interest in Blender and taught me basic skills.
This is the first video in a series of 14, but you learn enough to begin making your own art after the first few episodes

Human Modelling Tutorial

This is one of the first modelling tutorials I followed. Blender has a million different hotkeys and shortcuts and techniques for modelling, so there's a lot to learn.
This tutorial does a great job of teaching you an efficient modelling workflow even if you know little to nothing about Blender.

Basic 3D Animation Tutorial

This is a super short and easy to follow tutorial for the basics of animating things in blender. Animation is super interesting with lots of potential,
and I definitely want to get better with animating.
